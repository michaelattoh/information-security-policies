# Data Protection Policy

**Version:** 1.0  
**Author:** Michael Attoh  
**Effective Date:** March 2025  
**Classification:** Public  

## 1. Purpose
To ensure personal and company data is collected, processed, and stored securely in compliance with applicable data protection laws such as the **Data Protection Act, 2012 (Act 843)** and **GDPR** principles.

## 2. Scope
Covers all personal, financial, and operational data processed by the company, including employee, customer, and partner information.

## 3. Policy Statement
- Personal data shall be processed lawfully, fairly, and transparently.
- Data shall be collected only for specified, legitimate purposes and not retained longer than necessary.
- Appropriate technical and organizational measures (encryption, access controls, secure backups) must be applied to protect data.
- Data breaches must be reported to the Data Protection Officer within **24 hours** of discovery.
- Third-party processors must comply with equivalent data protection obligations.

## 4. Rights of Data Subjects
Individuals have the right to access, rectify, erase, or restrict processing of their personal data as permitted by law.

## 5. Enforcement
Non-compliance may result in disciplinary action and notification to regulatory authorities.

## 6. Review Cycle
Reviewed annually or when required by legal or operational changes.
